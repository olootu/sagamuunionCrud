Our story

Sagamu Union (GB) is an association of Sagamu indigenes residing in the UK. Founded in 2000, our main objectives are to cater for the welfare of our members, to embark on different projects that will uplift the standard of living of our people back home and also to promote our great town, SAGAMU.

Our meetings are held on every 3rd Saturday of the month. Who can join? Membership is open to all Sagamu indigenes throughout the UK and Ireland.
